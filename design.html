About me

My name is Nicole le Fevre and I am 24 years old. I live in Odense with my husband and dog. I have a huge love for graphic design, content creation and user interface. I am incredibly interested in designing and coding user interface and user experience as well as app development. I develop and code websites in HTML, CSS, JS, SASS, WP, a bit of PHP. I also work with photography, video, graphic design, animations, illustrations, which very much reflects my choice of education, as here I gain competencies in both design and website building. I am incredibly positive about life and take any challenge as a strength that shapes who I am. Meet me on UCL's campus or feel free to contact me if collaboration is desired.
